üöÄ How to Embed on Your Website

Step 1: Add this single line of code to your website (before closing </body> tag):

Step 2: Customize with data attributes (optional):

<script src="https://your-domain.com/medidesk.js" data-hospital-id="your-hospital-id" data-position="bottom-right" data-theme="blue" data-auto-open="false" ></script>
